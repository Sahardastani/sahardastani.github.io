Sahar Dastani

Sahar Dastani

I am a PhD Student at Mila/ÉTS, advised by Prof. Christian Desrosiers and Prof. Hervé Lombaert. I am also a visiting researcher at Zebra. My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, and efficient neural network design. Specifically, I focus on designing robust and efficient neural networks that can generalize under real-world challenges, such as distribution shifts and geometric transformations.

Previously, I was a research assistant at CHU Sainte-Justine in Montreal, advised by Dr. Philippe Jouvet. I obtain my M.Sc. and B.Sc. in Computer Science from Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT) where I advised by Dr. M.Hassan Shirali-Shahreza and Dr. Mohammad Akbari.
